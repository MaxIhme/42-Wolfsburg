
## SSH
- `sudo service ssh restart`
- `sudo service ssh status`
- in `/etc/ssh/sshd_config` look for #Port 22 (change that and delete #)
- look for ports `ss -natp`

## Apparmor
- `sudo service apparmor status`

## vi
- Insert mode - `i`
- command mode `Esc`
- delete one char `d`
- delete one word (to the right) `dw`
- save `:w!`
- quit `:q!`
- both `:wq!`
